person 01 — About

Who I Am

I'm a Senior Software Engineer with 4.9+ years building production-grade Human Machine Interfaces for the automotive and medical industries. My work spans the full stack — from UX wireframes and visual design in Figma through to Qt/QML implementation and low-level Embedded C++ on STM32, i.MX6, MIMXRT1170, and i.MX8 platforms.

On the automotive side I've shipped Instrument Clusters, Infotainment Systems, and ADAS visualisation for leading OEMs, working in A-SPICE aligned Agile environments with Jenkins CI/CD and requirements tooling like Polarion.

In the medical domain I designed and built a Physiotherapy Laser Therapy device HMI end-to-end — including a custom UART framework with CRC validation, SQLite3 patient database, and safety-aware interaction design.

I care about the intersection where engineering discipline meets great UX: interfaces that are beautiful, legible, deterministic, and safe.
